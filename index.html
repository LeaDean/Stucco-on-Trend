About the Craft

Centuries of Technique,
Applied Today

Venetian plaster — marmorino and stucco lustro — is one of the most demanding wall finishes in existence. Each layer is hand-burnished to build depth, translucency, and that unmistakable living sheen that no paint can replicate.

At Stucco on Trend, every project is personally overseen by our master plasterers. We work exclusively with lime-based pigments, natural marbles, and traditional Venetian methods refined over fifteen years of bespoke residential and commercial work.

How We Work

Our Process

01

Consultation

We visit your space, understand your vision, and discuss finishes, textures, and colour. Every project begins with listening.

02

Sample Boards

Bespoke hand-applied sample boards are created so you can see and feel the finish in your own light before commitment.

03

Preparation

Surfaces are meticulously prepared. No shortcuts — the longevity of Venetian plaster depends entirely on the foundation beneath.

04

Application & Burnish

Multiple coats, hand-burnished between each. A process of days, not hours, that builds depth invisible in cheaper alternatives.
